Common Questions

F.A.Q.

What will the legal service cost?

You are entitled to know what your fee will be at your first visit, to the extent that we can reasonably inform you. At Sabarwal Law, we hold ourselves accountable to the values of honesty and integrity and encourage an open discussion about our fees.

Lawyers are paid under various fee arrangements:

  • Hourly fee: Fees based on hourly rate for services rendered.
  • Flat fee: A stated sum for agreed upon services.
  • Contingency fee: Fees based on recovery amount; no recovery means no fee.
Do you hold special office hours?

All office visits at Sabarwal Law are by appointment only. We advise each of our clients to schedule meetings during regular business hours. Special evening appointments can be arranged if necessary, with a surcharge applied.

Why do I need a Small Business Lawyer?

You need a small business lawyer to protect you and your rights. A well-drafted agreement is like a good insurance policy in the event of an unforeseen dispute. At Sabarwal Law we provide solid advice, identify significant risks and help negotiate terms that you may not be aware of otherwise.

Why can't I just use a Web service like LegalZoom?

Web services like LegalZoom are not law firms and cannot provide legal advice. The role of a good lawyer is more than providing forms—they are a trusted advisor who will strategize with you and help think through legal issues specific to your situation.

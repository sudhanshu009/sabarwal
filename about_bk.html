About Us

At Sabarwal Law we are committed to the enhancement of life within our community, through relationships with the individual. No matter how complex the problem, we promise to give you the dedication you deserve by helping you understand your legal rights and providing outstanding service and expertise. With personalized attention and insightful counsel, we are more than lawyers – we are your number one advocates.

We are fluent in honesty, integrity and diversity; these values being the core of our Practice and the relationships we create with our clients.

We are in the unique position of having diversified backgrounds and experiences, both inside and out of the legal industry, giving us a specialized point of view in all our practice areas. We provide multi-faceted representation, leaving you to focus on what you do best.

We believe that our responsibility extends beyond the legal field and are proud to be committed to causes that better society. The attorneys at Sabarwal Law aim to enrich lives, by serving in a range of organizations that make a difference to issues we all connect on.

Sanjay Sabarwal

Coming from a long history of participating in community service, Sanjay got involved in the legal field to continue his service to others. After graduating from UCLA with a degree in English and Political Science, Sanjay took a year off to do Americorps, where he taught English in Lexington, Kentucky at an underserved school. Sanjay has continued to display his passion for improving the lives of others throughout his professional career. He currently volunteers to speak at schools through the Youth Business Alliance Program and participates in giving out food to the homeless in Stanton, California with the Illumination Foundation.

Sanjay Sabarwal focuses his practice in the areas of Employment Law, Family Law and Personal Injury Law. In addition to his current practice focus, Mr. Sabarwal has experience in Business Law, Wage Compensation, Partnership Disputes and Mediation. Mr. Sabarwal has been the Co-Owner and House Counsel for Ziba Beauty for the past 15 years, where he is familiar with all business and legal aspects of running a chain of 14 Store Locations.

Mr. Sabarwal is a member of the American Bar Association, Los Angeles Bar Association, South Asian Bar Association and Orange County Bar Association. He also serves on the Advisory Board for Youth Business Alliance, which connects entrepreneurs to speak at underserved schools in Los Angeles County. He is also a board member for the Artesia Chamber of Commerce. Mr. Sabarwal is admitted to practice at all California State Courts.

Jemal Yarbrough

Jemal Yarbrough focuses his practice in the areas of Professional Negligence, Business & Commercial Litigation, Employment Law, Consumer Warranty, Family Law and Mediation. In addition to his current practice focus, Mr. Yarbrough has substantial experience in DUI Defense, Civil Rights Defense, Lemon Law and general Commercial Litigation matters.

Mr. Yarbrough has represented and advocated for companies such as Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC; CALPOP, Inc.; Unum Provident; Colonial Life & Accident Insurance Co.; Jefferson Pilot Financial; The Fairchild Corporation, First American Home Buyers Protection; ITT Industries; BP America, Inc.; Plastic Express; and the California cities of Los Angeles, Hawthorne, Long Beach, Riverside and Port Hueneme.

Mr. Yarbrough has been a member of the Defense Research Institute, American Bar Association, Los Angeles Bar Association and the John M. Langston Bar Association of Los Angeles. Mr. Yarbrough is admitted to practice before the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, all of the Federal Courts in California, as well as all California State Courts.

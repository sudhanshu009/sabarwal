About Us

Welcome to Sabarwal Law:

At Sabarwal Law, our mission is simple — to protect, guide, and advocate for the people and businesses in our community with dedication and care.

Legal challenges can feel overwhelming, but you don’t have to face them alone. No matter how complex your situation may be, we take the time to help you understand your rights, your options, and the best path forward. Our clients receive personalized attention, thoughtful strategy, and experienced representation every step of the way.

We believe great legal service is built on relationships. That’s why we treat every client with respect, honesty, and transparency — providing not only skilled legal counsel, but also the peace of mind that comes from knowing you have a trusted advocate in your corner.

Sabarwal Law, we are more than attorneys.
We are your partners, your protectors, and your strongest voice.

Our practice is rooted in integrity, diversity, and accountability. These values shape how we serve our clients and how we engage with the community around us.

With broad professional backgrounds and diverse life experiences, our team brings a well-rounded, strategic perspective to every case. This multi-faceted approach allows us to deliver comprehensive representation, so you can stay focused on what matters most — your family, your business, and your future.

Beyond the courtroom, we are proud to support initiatives and organizations that strengthen our community. We believe our responsibility extends beyond legal work — it includes making a meaningful difference in the lives of the people we serve.

When you choose Sabarwal Law, you choose commitment, clarity, and confidence.

Sanjay Sabarwal

Meet Sanjay Sabarwal:

Service has always been at the heart of Sanjay Sabarwal’s work.

Long before becoming an attorney, Sanjay was dedicated to helping others and strengthening the communities around him. after earning his degree in English and Political Science from UCLA, he committed a year to AmeriCorps, teaching English at an underserved school in Lexington, Kentucky. That experience reinforced what continues to guide his career today — using his skills and knowledge to advocate for people who need support most.

That same spirit of service carries into every client relationship.

Sanjay believes that every person deserves to feel heard, respected, and protected. He takes the time to understand each client’s unique situation, offering thoughtful guidance, clear communication, and steady representation from start to finish. his approach is simple: treat every case with care, integrity, and personal attention.

Outside the courtroom, Sanjay remains deeply involved in the community. He volunteers as a speaker through the Youth Business Alliance Program, mentoring students and aspiring entrepreneurs, and regularly serves individuals experiencing homelessness through the Illumination Foundation in Stanton, California. Giving back isn’t just something he does — it’s who he is.

Jemal Yarbrough

Jemal Yarbrough focuses his practice in the areas of Professional Negligence, Business & Commercial Litigation, Employment Law, Consumer Warranty, Family Law and Mediation. In addition to his current practice focus, Mr. Yarbrough has substantial experience in DUI Defense, Civil Rights Defense, Lemon Law and general Commercial Litigation matters.

Mr. Yarbrough has represented and advocated for companies such as Mercedes-Benz USA, LLC; CALPOP, Inc.; Unum Provident; Colonial Life & Accident Insurance Co.; Jefferson Pilot Financial; The Fairchild Corporation, First American Home Buyers Protection; ITT Industries; BP America, Inc.; Plastic Express; and the California cities of Los Angeles, Hawthorne, Long Beach, Riverside and Port Hueneme.

Mr. Yarbrough has been a member of the Defense Research Institute, American Bar Association, Los Angeles Bar Association and the John M. Langston Bar Association of Los Angeles. Mr. Yarbrough is admitted to practice before the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, all of the Federal Courts in California, as well as all California State Courts.
